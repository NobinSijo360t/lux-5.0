About Luxathon
Luxathon is a dynamic 24-hour illumination hackathon that challenges participants to fuse creativity and technology in crafting transformative lighting experiences. Bringing together individuals with diverse skills, from design and engineering to programming, Luxathon explores the intersection of traditional and cutting-edge lighting methods. Participants work collaboratively to conceptualize and implement projects that showcase the innovative potential of light, utilizing technologies like LED, smart lighting, and interactive installations.

The event culminates in a showcase where projects are judged based on creativity, technical innovation, and overall impact. Luxathon not only fosters community and cross-disciplinary collaboration but also serves as a platform for networking and skill development, propelling the field of illumination design forward. As a unique blend of rapid prototyping and aesthetic considerations, Luxathon inspires participants to redefine the possibilities of light in shaping our surroundings.
